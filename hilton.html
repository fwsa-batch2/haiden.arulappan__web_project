Steps from the Cipet bus stop, this upscale hotel is 8 km from the Ampa Skywalk shopping mall

and 14 km from Marina Beach in the city centre.

The contemporary rooms have wall-to-wall windows, Wi-Fi (fees may apply) and flat-screens,

plus minibars, desks, and tea and coffeemaking facilities.

Upgraded rooms and suites add access to a private lounge serving free breakfast and drinks.

Suites also include separate living rooms. Room service is available.

Parking is free. There are 5 eateries, including a 24-hour cafe and a rooftop bar with private gazebos.

Other amenities include a business centre, a fitness room and an infinity pool.
