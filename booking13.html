Roseate House

  • A 3-minute walk from a metro station, this chic high-end hotel is 3 km from
    Indira Gandhi International Airport and 11 km from Qutb Minar (minaret).

  • Featuring garden or city views, the polished rooms with contemporary decor
    have complimentary Wi-Fi and flat-screens, as well as rainfall showers.

  • Club level rooms offer 24-hour butler service and access to a private lounge
    with evening refreshments; suites add living rooms. Some provide free-standing tubs and airport views.

  • Amenities include a bar, 2 restaurants and a rooftop cafe. There’s a
    garden and an outdoor pool, as well as a cinema and a luxe spa.

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
