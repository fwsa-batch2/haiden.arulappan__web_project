ITC Sonar

  • A 2-minute walk from the nearest bus stop , this upscale hotel
    surrounded by gardens is 10 km from the Victoria Memorial and 8 km from the Indian Museum.

  • The sophisticated rooms and suites come with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen
    TVs and desks, plus tea and coffeemakers. Suites feature garden and water
    views, and come with separate living areas.

  • There's a ladies-only floor. Room service is available.
    Freebies include parking and a breakfast buffet.
    There are 6 restaurants, 1 of which serves Asian cuisine, as well as a bakery.

  • Other amenities include a spa with a gym and a beauty salon, an outdoor pool,
    a business centre and event space.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Queen

A room with a queen-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of Queen
Rooms are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Mini Suite

A single room with a bed and sitting area. Sometimes
the sleeping area is in a bedroom separate from the parlour or living room.

The room size or area of Junior Suites
are generally between 60 m² to 80 m².
