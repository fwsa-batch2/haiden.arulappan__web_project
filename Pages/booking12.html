Aloft Aerocity

  • In a commercial area, this contemporary hotel is 3 km from
    Indira Gandhi International Airport and 13 km from the Qutub Minar tower.

  • The sleek rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars,
    plus wet bars and marble en suite, shower-only bathrooms.

  • Upgraded rooms add pool views; some include access to a club lounge.
    Studios and apartments provide sitting areas with sofas. Room service is available 24/7.

  • Parking is complimentary. A breakfast buffet (fee) is served in a chic restaurant.
    There's also a hip bar featuring regular live music. Other amenities consist of an
    outdoor pool, a 24-hour gym and a spa, plus meeting rooms and event space.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Adjacent

Rooms close to each other,
perhaps across the hall.

The room size or area of Adjacent Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Hollywood Twin

A room that can accommodate two persons with two
twin beds joined together by a common headboard.

Most of the budget hotels tend to provide many
of these room settings which cater both couples and parties in two.
