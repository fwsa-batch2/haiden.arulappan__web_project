Hayatt Regency

  • Off NH1, this upscale hotel is a 5-minute walk
    from Salt Lake Stadium metro station.

  • The polished rooms feature teak furnishings, floor-to-ceiling
    windows and Italian marble bathrooms. They offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and desks.

  • Upgraded rooms and suites add sitting areas, airport transfers and
    private lounge access; some have separate living rooms, balconies,
    kitchenettes and plush meeting rooms. Room service is available.

  • There are 2 restaurants offering Italian or North Indian cuisine,
    as well as a cafe and 2 bars. There's also an outdoor pool,
    a spa and a gym, as well as meeting and conference facilities.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Adjacent

Rooms close to each other,
perhaps across the hall.

The room size or area of Adjacent Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Hollywood Twin

A room that can accommodate two persons with two
twin beds joined together by a common headboard.

Most of the budget hotels tend to provide many
of these room settings which cater both couples and parties in two.
