JW Marriot sahar

  • Across from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport,
    this luxe hotel is 5 km from the Mahakali Caves and 7 km from Powai Lake.

  • Plush rooms have Wi-Fi (fee), flat-screens and DVD players,
    as well as iPod docks, minibars and coffeemakers.

  • Upgraded rooms add pool views and/or balconies; club rooms
    offer access to a private lounge. Suites add living areas;
    upgraded suites have dining areas. Room service is offered.

  • Parking is free. There are international and Italian restaurants,
    along with a bright bar/cafe, a posh lounge and a deli.
    Other amenities include an outdoor pool, a hot tub and a gym,
    plus a spa, a business centre and 11 meeting rooms.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Queen

A room with a queen-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of Queen
Rooms are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Studio

A room with a studio bed- a couch which can be
converted into a bed. May also have an additional bed.

The room size or area of Studio room
types are generally between 25 m² to 40 m².
