Intercontinental

  • With direct beach access, this sleek, high-end resort is an 11-minute walk from
    the Madras Crocodile Bank Trust and 10 km from the Tiger Cave rock-cut temple.

  • Chic, contemporary rooms come with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs,
    plus daybeds, sunken tubs and rainfall showers.

  • Upgraded rooms have lotus pond or pool views, and suites add
    balconies and separate living areas. Room service is available.

  • Freebies include breakfast and parking. There's a Chinese restaurant, an open-air
    cafe set on a floating deck, and a 1920s American-style bar.
    Other amenities include an outdoor pool, an Ayurvedic spa and landscaped gardens.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Queen

A room with a queen-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of Queen
Rooms are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Mini Suite

A single room with a bed and sitting area. Sometimes
the sleeping area is in a bedroom separate from the parlour or living room.

The room size or area of Junior Suites
are generally between 60 m² to 80 m².
