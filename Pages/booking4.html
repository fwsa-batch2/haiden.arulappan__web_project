Taj Santacruz

  • Adjoining the domestic terminals of Chhatrapati Shivaji International
    Airport, this lavish hotel is 7 km from the Bandra Kurla Complex business district.

  • Sleek rooms feature flat-screens, Bluetooth speakers and Wi-Fi,
    as well as safes, and tea and coffeemakers. Some have runway views;

  • suites add walk-in closets, and sitting areas or living rooms.
    Club level quarters have access to a lounge serving free
    snacks and cocktails. Room service is available 24/7.

  • There are 4 refined restaurants and bars, including a Chinese eatery
    with show cooking. Other amenities include a spa, a 24-hour
    gym and an outdoor pool, as well as meeting/event spaces.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Queen

A room with a queen-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of Queen
Rooms are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Mini suite

A single room with a bed and sitting area. Sometimes
the sleeping area is in a bedroom separate from the parlour or living room.

The room size or area of Junior Suites
are generally between 60 m² to 80 m².
