The Leela Palace

  • Overlooking the beach, this stately hotel with a Colonial-style
    exterior is 8 km from the Valluvar Kottam monument
    and 10 km from Chennai Central railway station.

  • Featuring hardwood floors and marble bathrooms, the posh rooms
    offer free Wi-Fi, 102-cm flat-screen TVs and Blu-ray players;
    most have ocean views. Suites add living rooms, and some include kitchenettes.

  • Room service is available. Freebies include parking and a breakfast buffet.
    There are upscale Indian and Italian eateries, plus a lobby bar, a patisserie,

  • And a chic Chinese restaurant serving dim sum brunch.
    Additional amenities include an outdoor pool, a spa and a gym.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Adjacent

Rooms close to each other,
perhaps across the hall.

The room size or area of Adjacent Room Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Hollywood Twin

A room that can accommodate two persons with two
twin beds joined together by a common headboard.

Most of the budget hotels tend to provide many
of these room settings which cater both couples and parties in two.
