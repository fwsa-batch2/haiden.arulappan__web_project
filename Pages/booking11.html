Shangri_la Eros

  • A 5-minute walk from Patel Chowk metro station,
    this sophisticated high-rise hotel is 2 km from
    India Gate monument and 7 km from 17th-century Red Fort.

  • Plush rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars,
    plus sitting areas and tea and coffeemaking facilities.

  • Upgraded rooms add city views. Suites include separate living areas,
    balconies, whirlpool baths and/or kitchenettes. There's 24-hour room service.

  • There's a buffet restaurant, an Italian eatery and a Chinese restaurant.
    Other amenities include an outdoor pool and a hot tub, plus a sauna,
    an exercise room and a spa. Breakfast (surcharge) and parking are available.

King

A room with a king-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of King Rooms
are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Queen

A room with a queen-sized bed.
May be occupied by one or more people.

The room size or area of Queen
Rooms are generally between 32 m² to 50 m².

Cabana

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool
or have a private pool attached to the room.

The room size or area of Cabana Room
Types are generally between 30 m² to 45 m².

Hollywood Twin

A room that can accommodate two persons with two
twin beds joined together by a common headboard.

Most of the budget hotels tend to provide many
of these room settings which cater both couples and parties in two.
