Novotel

  • In a modern building off State Highway 49A, this casual hotel is an 18-minute walk
    from Prathyangira Devi Temple, 5 km from VGP Universal
    Kingdom theme park and 6 km from Golden Beach.

  • The airy rooms with a contemporary vibe come with complimentary Wi-Fi,
    flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemaking facilities.

  • Suites add separate living rooms and city views.
    Room service is available 24/7.Amenities include a

  • low-key lobby bar, a fitness centre and 10 meeting rooms.
    There's also a relaxed international restaurant; breakfast is offered for a fee.
